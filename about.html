About Me

I love nature because I grew up living in parks, hiking, playing in creeks, and wandering through the woods until sunset. These early adventures shaped who I am today and continue to inspire every piece of art I create and every story I tell.

With a degree in biology and a master's in education, I've combined my passion for the natural world with my love for teaching and creativity. My goal is to share the beauty and joy that comes from becoming aware of what's around us—to help others take joy in everyday observations of the world.

I've been creating art since I was old enough to hold a crayon, always thinking of new ways to be creative. Inspired by my mother, who is also an artist, I want to share the adventures and whimsy in my head with others, sparking joy and creativity in everyone I reach.

Through my watercolors, photography, and storytelling, I hope to invite you into a world where the natural and fantastical intertwine—where a walk in the woods becomes an adventure, and every creek holds a secret waiting to be discovered.
