Books & Writing

I create stories that celebrate nature, spark imagination, and inspire cozy adventures for readers of all ages.

Children's Books

Focus: Beautifully illustrated books centered on the outdoors and natural spaces, designed to help young readers connect with nature through art and story.

Current Projects:

  • A picture book celebrating life in Virginia state parks—where adventures begin at every trail
  • Gulliver's Adventures series—featuring anthropomorphic pets who embark on whimsical journeys and converse with wild animals. These stories are for slightly older elementary readers with more text and cozy adventures.

Young Adult Fantasy

I'm currently crafting a young adult fantasy novel that weaves together magic, nature, and coming-of-age themes. This story invites readers into a world where the natural and fantastical intertwine.

Stay tuned for updates on upcoming releases and illustrations!
